Ayushman Bharat Yogna



The uncertainties in life have led to the growth of the healthcare sector in India. There has been a subsequent rise in the demand for health insurance plans over the past three decades. Due to this reason, the government of India has introduced the Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana Scheme or Ayushman Bharat Yojana. This is a flagship program launched by the government, offering financial protection to the poor in case of unannounced medical exigency. The authority’s main aim is to increase the health expenditure to 2.5 percent of the country’s GDP by the year 2025. Read this blog to know more about PMJAY Scheme or Ayushman Bharat Yojana. Ayushman Bharat Yojana is introduced to provide health coverage to approximately 50 crore citizens in India and the average amount to the beneficiary is INR 5 lakhs. The scheme covers the majority of diagnostics, medicines, pre-hospitalization costs, and medical treatment expenses.
